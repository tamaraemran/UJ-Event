UJ Event Project

UJ Event was created to bring the University of Jeddah community closer together by gathering all campus activities in one reliable place. It helps students easily discover and join events that match their interests without the hassle of searching through crowded emails. At the same time, it provides organizers with a simple tool to manage registrations and reach the right audience effectively.

Technologies Used:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • Node.js
  • MySQL
